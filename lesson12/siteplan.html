Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website it to provide visitors information about the History and current demographics about Marshfield, WI. It is also to provide information about local businesses and current events as well as invite local people to become members of it's chamber of commerace.

Target Audience

My target audience is men and women 35-50 years old.

Ashlee Person is a 37 year old female business owner. She just started up her business a year ago and is wanting to advertise more. She has a family and friends in the community and would like her business to be featured on the Marshfield Chamber of Commerace Page and become a member herself.

Joe Dagget is a 42 year old local business owner that has been a part of the community for the last 10 years. He has been a member of the Chamber of Commerace the entire time and wants to continue to build his business relationships.
