About

In 2020, before the word pandemic was universally known, we had an idea that involved going back to school. With a passion for interior design, dusting off old backpacks, and a hope for something great we started working towards Remembering June. Here we discuss our passion for making ANY home an inviting and beautiful place to be. Most people spend the majority of their time in the four walls called home, and we want to help you make yours a place you love to be.

Alexa lives in South Jordan, UT with her husband Neil, four of her children, and their Yorkie Maple. Adrienne lives in Dallas, GA with her husband Kaleb, three children, and nothing else that needs to be kept alive.
