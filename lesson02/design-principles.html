Alignment

Jane

Visit Jane

This website by Jane shows the design principle of Alignment really well. The margins are aligned really nicely all the way down the left side of the page, except for the banner which helps provide contrast and draws the eye to that area.

Fitt's Law

House of Beauty

Visit House of Beauty

The House of Beauty website provides a great example of Fitt's Law. The bright red Shop Now button is very visable and big enough to know if you click there you will be able to begin shopping for the gift set above. The little arrow icons on the sides of the products also are easily identifiable, showing how you can switch to the next product and back.

White Space and Clean Design

BTS

Visit BTS

Here we can see how nice and clean of a design this website has. The simple white square with the contrasting BE, the name of the album, in black font, and the pretty script font beneath. The black background contrasts again with the large white square and gives lots of white space to draw your eye to what's most important.
